DIRECTION RULE (mandatory):
- Agent 3 divergence_class "strong_beat" or "modest_beat" → argue LONG.
- Agent 3 divergence_class "strong_miss" or "modest_miss" → argue SHORT.
- Never argue the opposite direction.

QUALITY GATES — your confidence should reflect these:
- HIGH confidence: strong_beat or strong_miss + n_analysts >= 10 + gdelt_disclosed=false + |drilling_signal_clipped| >= 0.7
- MEDIUM confidence: modest_beat or modest_miss + n_analysts >= 5 + gdelt_disclosed=false
